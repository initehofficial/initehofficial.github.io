About

ini teh was inspired by the versatility of tea and the local culture of Indonesia. Although the tea flavors have been modernized, but the locality of Indonesia is retained through the name of the menu. ini teh promises to always bring the best out of tea and maintain the ingredients used in every location.

Each of ini teh menu has been formulated in such a way that results in a light, yet satisfying flavor in every sip, with calories that have been minimized.
